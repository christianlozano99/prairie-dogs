Different types of domesticated prairie dog breeds and their descriptions:

Black-tailed Prairie Dog
Black tailed prairie dogs are one of the most common prairie dogs.

White-tailed Prairie Dog
These prairie dogs are also known as "chiselers".

Gunnison's Prairie Dog
Gunnison's coats are yellow-toned buff merged with hairs that are black colored.

Utah Prairie Dog
This prairie dog is actually the smallest species of a prairie dog and it is part of the squirrel family.

Mexican Prairie Dog
This prairie dog is endangered because it is considered an "agricultural pest".
